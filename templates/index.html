CSE1321/L Grade Calculator

Following rules are used to calculate lab grade:
- Average your lab exercise grades (the lowest is dropped). These are worth 10% of your overall grade.
- Average your assignment grades (the lowest is dropped). These are worth 40% of your overall grade
- Your midterm grade is worth 20%
- Your final exam grade is worth 30%.
Note, if your final exam grade is higher than your midterm grade, it replaces your midterm grade.
All bonus points for Tutoring are added to your final exam grades, before we do replacement of Midterms.

Leave boxes empty if scores are not available. Missing entries are treated as 0 for min grade and 100 for max grade.
If all boxes are filled you will know the exact grade according to above mentioned ditributions
